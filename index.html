Oh the Places I've Been

I love to travel and experience new places. I am not the most advanced traveler, but I have been to some wonderful places! Now I will share some of my experiences.

  • Calgary Canada

    • The trip to Calgary included a few days at the Stampede. At the Stampede I experienced my first rodeo!

  • Aruba

    • Aruba has beautiful relaxing beaches.

  • Sydney, Australia

    • The journey to Australia started with 30 hours of traveling. In Sydney I climbed the famous bridge and the trip wrapped up with the closing fireworks to the 2000 Olympics!

  • Rome, Italy

    • The trip to Rome was filled with history. My favorite part was the tour of St. Peter's- just amazing!
